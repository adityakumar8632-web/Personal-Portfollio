Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers.

How long does it take to build a website?

Timelines are designed to balance speed with quality avoiding rushed decisions that create long-term issues..

What's your development process?

I follow an iterative approach: discovery and planning, design mockups, development in sprints, regular feedback sessions, testing and quality assurance, and finally deployment. You'll be involved throughout the entire process.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, because most problems appear after launch, not before.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes, all websites I build are fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. With over 60% of web traffic coming from mobile, ensuring a seamless mobile experience is a top priority.

What technologies do you work with?

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Next.js, Node.js, TypeScript, and various CSS frameworks. I choose the best tech stack based on your project's specific requirements and goals.

How much does a website cost?

Project costs vary based on scope, complexity, and features. Simple websites start around $2,000, while complex applications can range from $10,000+. I provide detailed quotes after our initial consultation.
